About me!
Tobias Demetz

Heyy, I am Tobias Demetz. :))
I am 19 years old and come from Val Gardena. Since I was 6 years old, I have been very passionated for filmmaking and created advertising films, short films, documentary films, science films, travel films, films with vfx, etc.

In 2019 I did an exchange year in Ireland where I my interest for filmmaking rised.

In 2020 I had the opportunity to work as pre-editor in the film "Resilient", directed by Roberto Faenza and produced by Elda Ferri. The costume designer Milena Canonero has won 4 oscars and one of the actors was Laura Haddock, who acted in Transformers, Da Vinci's Demons, Guardians of the Galaxy,...

In the following months I bought a drone, a green screen set, an e-bike, a 32-inch Monitor and started to film as much as possible.

I am most into movies, but also into nature documentaries, advertising videos and travelling videos.

For more information or for a collaboration feel free to contact me filling out the form below ;))
